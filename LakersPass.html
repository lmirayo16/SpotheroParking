Getting There

Enter At:

820 South Grand Avenue, Los Angeles, CA
Enter this location at 820 S Grand Ave. This is the 820 S Grand Ave. lot, operated by Unified Parking. It is located on the left-hand side of S Grand Ave.(a one-way street) between W 8th St. and W 9th St. The entrance is immediately before Uncle John's Cafe and The Stillwell Hotel.
Navigate There

Things You Should Know

  • Due to size constraints, this location is unable to accommodate pickup trucks and passenger vans.
  • Please note that this facility may become valet if occupancy is high.
  • Convenient and inexpensive option for USC and Rams games at Los Angeles Memorial Coliseum. This parking facility is only a 6-minute walk from 7th St./Metro Center stop, where the Metro Blue line will take you directly to the Expo/Vermont station at the stadium. Rail schedule: https://www.metro.net/riding/maps/
  • Make sure to buy your Metro round-trip fare for $3.50 at the beginning of your journey. SpotHero will reimburse you with a $5 parking credit! Use the code LAFOOTBALL to get $5 off your reservation.
  • The license plate on your parking pass must match the vehicle's license plate. Failure to add the license plate may result in a tow.
